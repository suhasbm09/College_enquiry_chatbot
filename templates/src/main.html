About Us

The National Institute of Engineering (NIE), established in 1946, is a prestigious grant-in-aid institution ranked in the 151–200 band among India’s top engineering colleges by NIRF 2023 and 17th by The Week. Managed by a society with nine out of ten directors being alumni, NIE is autonomous under Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and approved by AICTE. Accredited by NAAC, we offer 7 UG and 10 PG programs, with most courses accredited under NBA Tier-I. NIE operates from two campuses in Mysuru and hosts 15 Centres of Excellence for research and consultancy in collaboration with industries and alumni. Recognized under MHRD-World Bank TEQIP across three phases, it also functions as a research center under VTU and AICTE. The institute fosters holistic student development with strong academics, robust faculty, quality housing, vibrant campus life, and excellent sports and cultural opportunities.

Principal's Message

The National Institute of Engineering (NIE) has been at the forefront of technical education in Karnataka, through the continuous implementation of Outcome Based Education (OBE), ensuring that our graduates are able to face up to the challenges demanded by the engineering profession.

- Dr. Rohini Nagapadma
